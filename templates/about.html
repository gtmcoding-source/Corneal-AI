About The Product

Built to make high-quality learning output consistent

AI Notes Maker started with one problem: most learners spend too much time creating notes and too little time revising them. Our platform fixes that ratio.

Mission

Give every learner and educator a fast, structured, and reliable note workflow from any content source.

Vision

Become the default AI note infrastructure for students, test-prep centers, and modern learning teams.

Core product strengths

Multi-source ingestion

Text, PDFs, web pages, YouTube, and files all flow into one unified summary output.

Business-ready auth

OAuth support for Gmail and GitHub with flexible login options for operational scale.

Plan and billing workflow

Starter, Pro, Team, and Institution with coupon-ready payment journey.

Export and retention

Saved history and PDF exports for review workflows and long-term study tracking.
